Compact Integrated Fixed-Film Activated Sludge
System + Outside Membrane Bio-Reactor

The CIFAS + OMBR system is a compact, high-efficiency wastewater treatment solution that combines fixed-film activated sludge with external membrane filtration for superior contaminant removal. Designed to eliminate traditional clarifiers and sand filters, it reduces space requirements and operational complexity while ensuring high-quality effluent. Ideal for industrial and municipal use, it offers a cost-effective and sustainable approach to wastewater management.

Key Features of CIFAS + OMBR

Compact Design

Guaranteed DENR DAO 2016-08 and 2021-19 Compliance

No Sand Filter or Traditional Clarifier

Low Operation Cost Compared to Conventional STP
